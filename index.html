CornerSpace

An online voxel-art making platform powered by WebGL, Amplify, and Next.js. Supports realistic ray-traced rendering, an intuitive UI, user login, and the ability to share/like voxel art with other users.

3 Body Problem Simulation

A simulation of 3 masses and the effects of their gravitational pulls on each other. Project inspired by Liu Cixin sci-fi novel The Three-Body Problem.
